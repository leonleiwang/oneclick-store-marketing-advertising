# Deliverable Specs

Use these specs to plan practical assets. Exact dimensions may vary by printer or platform; include editable copy so the owner or designer can adjust.

## Print Assets

### Door / Storefront Poster

- Common ratios: A3 vertical, A2 vertical, 4:5 vertical.
- Viewing distance: 1-5 meters.
- Copy: one dominant headline, one offer line, one date/location/CTA line.
- Visual: product or service result must dominate.
- Avoid: dense paragraphs, tiny QR-code-only CTA, low contrast.

### Roll-up Banner

- Common ratio: 80x200 cm vertical or 2:5 vertical.
- Viewing distance: 2-8 meters.
- Copy: headline at eye level, 3 benefit/offer bullets, bottom CTA.
- Visual: tall hierarchy, clear top-to-bottom scan.

### Window Ad

- Common ratios: wide landscape, vertical decal, square sticker groups.
- Viewing distance: outside the store.
- Copy: shortest of all assets. Make it readable while walking.
- CTA: "进店看新品", "今晚可预约", "扫码入群领抽奖券".

### Price Tag / Shelf Talker

- Common ratios: 3:2, 4:3, small vertical.
- Copy: product name, mini hook, price/offer, optional tag like "新品" or "人气".
- Visual: product cutout or small icon; high contrast.
- Rule: no more than 2 short lines plus price.

### Flyer / Event Page

- Common ratios: A5 vertical, 4:5 social/event hybrid.
- Copy: event name, who it is for, time, rules, prize/benefit, CTA.
- Include: staff checklist or setup notes separately, not on the public flyer.

## Social Assets

### Xiaohongshu

- Preferred visual: 3:4 or 4:5 cover, strong title text.
- Caption: hook first line, visit reason, what to buy/do, address placeholder, save/comment CTA.
- Tone: specific, experience-based, lightly conversational.

### WeChat Moments

- Visual: square or 4:5.
- Copy: shorter than Xiaohongshu. One hook, one offer, one address/time line.
- CTA: comment, DM, scan QR, visit today.

### Instagram

- Visual: 1:1, 4:5, or story 9:16.
- Copy: concise English or bilingual if the audience needs it.
- CTA: visit, tag friend, save, story reply.

### Discord / WeChat Group Announcement

- Copy: time-sensitive, concrete, community-native.
- Include: who can join, what happens, start time, reward, registration rule.

## Product / Lifestyle Images

- Use original or generic products unless authorized branded/IP assets are provided.
- For physical products, request reference photos if visual consistency matters.
- For text-heavy assets, keep text editable in markdown and use image text as placeholder.

## Default Asset Type Mapping

- `poster`: main poster, A3/A2, 1024x1536 or 1536x2048.
- `rollup`: roll-up banner, 1024x2048.
- `window`: window ad, 1536x1024 or 1024x1536.
- `price-tag`: shelf talker or price card, 1024x768.
- `event-page`: flyer/event page, 1024x1536.
- `social`: social square or 4:5, 1024x1024 or 1024x1280.
- `product`: product/lifestyle image, 1024x1024.

## Prompt Quality Checklist

Each prompt should include:

- Store category and campaign goal.
- Exact asset type and intended viewing context.
- One dominant visual subject.
- Chinese text placeholders and separate editable copy.
- Shared campaign style lock.
- Size/aspect ratio.
- "High contrast, readable hierarchy, uncluttered composition."
- IP and factual constraints when relevant.
