# Industry Playbooks

Use these playbooks to make campaigns feel native to the store category. Pick the closest category, then adapt to the actual brief.

## Anime Merch / Guzi Store

Buyer psychology:

- Collection, rarity, same-fandom identity, surprise, social sharing, "I found it first".
- Strong triggers: new drop, blind box opening, hidden item, photo wall, campus after-class visit, friend group visit.

Best mechanics:

- New drop wall: first pick window, limited quantity labels, "arrive early, choose first".
- Stamp rally: one stamp per purchase/check-in; full card gets draw chance.
- Same-fandom group visit: bring a friend for a small gift or extra draw ticket.
- Mystery bag hour: short time window with transparent rules.
- Photo spot challenge: original mascot, display wall, hashtag/check-in.

Copy direction:

- Short, playful, fandom-native, energetic.
- Use words like "同担", "拆谷", "上新", "隐藏款", "谷子快乐", "先到先挑" when appropriate.
- Avoid naming protected characters unless the user provides authorized assets.

Visual direction:

- Bright retail lighting, display shelves, acrylic stand-like silhouettes, badges, cards, stickers, original mascot shapes.
- Use original cute/street anime-inspired graphics, not copyrighted characters or official logos.

## Figure / Collectible Store

Buyer psychology:

- Display pride, craftsmanship, scarcity, condition, authenticity, collector trust.

Best mechanics:

- Preorder wall, collector night, display contest, member preview, limited reservation perk.

Copy direction:

- More premium and precise than guzi stores.
- Emphasize details, texture, scale, display scene, collector-grade care.

Visual direction:

- Dramatic spotlight, glass cabinet, clean dark/light contrast, close-ups of sculpting and paint texture.

## DND / Tabletop RPG Store

Buyer psychology:

- Belonging, story, weekly ritual, low barrier for newcomers, campaign continuity.

Best mechanics:

- New adventurer night, one-shot table, DM recruitment, dice draw, character creation session.

Copy direction:

- Welcoming and imaginative.
- Reduce intimidation: "新手可来", "不用自带队伍", "现场带你开卡".

Visual direction:

- Tabletop map, dice, character sheets, warm tavern mood, readable event schedule.

## Board Game Store

Buyer psychology:

- Social fun, date/friend gathering, easy entry, variety, replay value.

Best mechanics:

- Newcomer table, party game night, group package, ranking board, family afternoon.

Copy direction:

- Low-pressure, group-oriented, time-specific.
- Emphasize "tonight has a table", "bring friends", "staff teaches".

Visual direction:

- Real table scene, hands, cards/tokens, laughter cues, clear time/price panels.

## Pet Store

Buyer psychology:

- Care, trust, cuteness, safety, pet comfort, owner pride.

Best mechanics:

- Pet birthday month, grooming/try-on day, photo wall, sample gift, membership refill card.

Copy direction:

- Warm, trustworthy, specific.
- Avoid medical claims without proof.

Visual direction:

- Bright clean store, happy pets, product close-ups, soft but not childish palette.

## Coffee Shop

Buyer psychology:

- Daily ritual, taste, atmosphere, work/study spot, social check-in.

Best mechanics:

- Morning set, study hour refill, tasting flight, seasonal drink launch, stamp card.

Copy direction:

- Sensory and concise: aroma, temperature, texture, routine.
- Tie offer to a time of day.

Visual direction:

- Real cup, steam, counter, pastry pairing, street/window context, warm light.

## Barbershop / Hair Salon

Buyer psychology:

- Transformation, confidence, trust, trend fit, appointment scarcity.

Best mechanics:

- New client slot perk, student cut day, before/after wall, stylist recommendation menu, referral gift.

Copy direction:

- Clear service, price range, booking CTA.
- Avoid overpromising transformation outcomes.

Visual direction:

- Clean portraits, chair/mirror tools, before/after layout if real photos are provided, strong typography.

## Nail Salon

Buyer psychology:

- Self-expression, mood, seasonality, social photo, appointment rhythm.

Best mechanics:

- New design drop, bestie set, seasonal palette, member refill, lookbook wall.

Copy direction:

- Style names matter: milk tea, jelly, chrome, ribbon, y2k, clean girl, dark sweet, etc.
- CTA should drive booking.

Visual direction:

- Macro hand shots, palette swatches, clean backgrounds, appointment card layout.
