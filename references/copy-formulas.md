# Copy Formulas

Use these formulas to create category-native Chinese marketing copy quickly. Keep copy concrete and local.

## Headline Patterns

### New Product Launch

- `新品已到，先到先挑`
- `今天的快乐，来自[核心动作]`
- `[人群]的本周新宝藏`
- `[场景]前，先来店里补给`
- `这次上新，值得绕路来一趟`

### Foot Traffic

- `路过就进来看看`
- `今天进店，有小惊喜`
- `[地点/人群]附近的新去处`
- `下课/下班后，来这里回血`

### Event Turnout

- `[时间]，一起开局`
- `新手也能来的[主题]夜`
- `带朋友来，多一次抽奖机会`
- `今晚有桌，缺你一个`

### Basket Size

- `凑一套，更有感觉`
- `第二件更适合送朋友`
- `组合入手，快乐翻倍`
- `满[门槛]，解锁[奖励]`

### Repeat Visit / Membership

- `每来一次，都离奖励更近`
- `集满[数量]章，换一份小快乐`
- `会员日，留给熟客的惊喜`

## Social Caption Structure

### Xiaohongshu

```text
[强钩子标题]

今天在[地点/店铺类型]发现一个很适合[人群]的[活动/新品]。
[具体亮点1]，[具体亮点2]，而且[到店理由/限时理由]。

适合：
1. [人群/场景]
2. [人群/场景]
3. [人群/场景]

[时间/地址/规则]
想看实拍/清单可以评论[关键词]。
```

### WeChat Moments

```text
[活动/新品]来了。
[一句核心卖点]。
[时间/优惠/参与方式]，路过可以进店看看。
```

### Discord / WeChat Group

```text
@everyone
[活动名] 本周[时间]开始。
适合：[新手/老客/同好/朋友组队]
规则：[1-3条]
奖励：[具体奖励]
报名/到店方式：[方式]
```

## Offer Formulas

- `进店礼`: no-purchase small gift or draw ticket.
- `好友礼`: bring a friend for both-side reward.
- `满额礼`: threshold reward that increases basket size.
- `集章`: repeat visit mechanism; make the reward visible.
- `限时`: time window, not fake scarcity.
- `预约`: service stores should convert interest into a slot.
- `社群`: scan/join for drop notice, event table, lookbook, or member perks.

## Event Mechanics

### Stamp Rally

- Goal: repeat visits and collection motivation.
- Rules: one stamp per visit/purchase; bonus stamp for friend or social post.
- Reward: small guaranteed reward + bigger draw chance.
- Staff needs: stamp card, stamp, prize box, rule card.

### Lucky Draw

- Goal: immediate excitement and social sharing.
- Rules: one draw for purchase/check-in/joining group; clear prize tiers.
- Reward: small rewards should be common; top prize should be visible.
- Staff needs: draw box, prize list, record sheet.

### Newcomer Night

- Goal: remove barrier for first-time visitors.
- Rules: fixed time, staff teaches, no experience required.
- Reward: participation gift or member coupon.
- Staff needs: host, signup sheet, starter kit.

### Group Buy

- Goal: basket size and friend referral.
- Rules: 2/3/4 people unlock escalating reward.
- Reward: gift, discount, extra draw, upgrade.
- Staff needs: price card, group verification rule.

## Staff Script Formula

```text
欢迎进店，今天是[活动]。
如果你是[人群/兴趣]，可以先看[推荐区域]。
现在[参与方式]可以拿到[奖励/抽奖/集章]。
要不要我帮你挑一个[低门槛推荐]？
```

## Anti-generic Rules

Avoid:

- "品质生活，从这里开始"
- "给你不一样的体验"
- "好物值得拥有"
- "精彩活动等你来"

Replace with:

- A specific product/action.
- A specific audience.
- A specific time window.
- A specific reward.
- A specific reason to enter or book now.
